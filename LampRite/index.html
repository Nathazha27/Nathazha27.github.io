Description

A first-person psychological horror and investigation game featuring a minimal defense system but no combat.

You play as a priest sent to investigate a grand Victorian mansion, where something deeply unsettling has taken root. As you uncover the truth behind the events that unfolded within its walls, you will realize that some secrets were never meant to be revealed.

Armed with a Sacred Lamp, you can reveal hidden secrets and clues... but its light exposes you to unseen dangers lurking in the darkness.

Will you be able to keep your faith intact, or will you succumb to the influence of the malevolent entity lurking in the shadows?

Key Features

Reveal the Unseen

Use your most powerful tool to uncover hidden and paranormal elements invisible to the human eye. This unique mechanic allows you to solve complex puzzles and discover fragments of the true story as you navigate through absolute darkness.

Paranormal Investigation

Become a true investigator without intrusive interfaces, reconstructing the narrative through clues, notes, and supernatural visions. Face constant paranormal activity that alters the environment and forces you to adapt your strategy to survive.

Experience the Victorian Era

Immerse yourself in a realistic and oppressive experience set in 1870s England, where every piece of furniture and room has been crafted with historical accuracy. Dynamic lighting and an unsettling soundscape create a distinctive dark and religious atmosphere.

Faith System

Manage your faith carefully; prolonged use of the lamp will drain your sanity and reveal your position to the enemy. You must seek out the house’s altars to perform restoration rituals before madness consumes your soul permanently.
